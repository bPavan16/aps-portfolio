Data Collection & Validation Methodology

Performance Metrics Collection

  • All latency measurements represent 99th percentile (P99) values
  • Throughput metrics are sustained performance under normal load
  • Efficiency improvements are compared to industry baselines
  • Business impact figures are conservative estimates based on publicly available data

Data Validation Process

  • Cross-referenced multiple independent sources for accuracy
  • Used conservative estimates when exact figures unavailable
  • Validated algorithm complexity against theoretical analysis
  • Verified business metrics against industry reports and academic literature
